Frequently Asked Questions

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $100. For orders under $100, standard shipping is $10. We also offer express shipping (2-3 business days) for $20 and next-day delivery for $35.

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by visiting our Track Order page and entering your order number.

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We're working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations in the near future.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Cash on Delivery for eligible orders. All payments are processed securely.
